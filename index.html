A controlled, four-step academic workflow

Designed to reduce uncertainty and align deliverables with academic expectations.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    Requirements, template/rubric, and deadline are reviewed; scope and milestones confirmed.

  2. 02

    Assignment

    Work begins with research planning and structured drafting or targeted editing support.

  3. 03

    Review

    Quality checks for structure, coherence, referencing, and format alignment.

  4. 04

    Delivery

    Final deliverables shared with revision notes and next-step guidance when requested.
