What HEC compliance means

HEC compliance generally refers to alignment with formatting, referencing, and methodological expectations commonly adopted by Pakistani universities.

Formatting & referencing alignment

Support includes margin settings, font usage, headings, table/figure formatting, and consistent application of approved citation styles.

Methodology & structure review

Ensuring coherence between research questions, objectives, design, instruments, and analysis planning.

Supervisor-aligned revisions

Feedback is mapped directly to supervisor or examiner comments to support efficient revisions.
