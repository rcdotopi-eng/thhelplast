HEC Thesis Submission Checklist (Pakistan)

This checklist summarizes common requirements observed across Pakistani universities following HEC-aligned thesis standards. Always confirm with your department.

1. Document Structure

  • Title page approved by department
  • Abstract within prescribed word limit
  • Table of contents, list of figures, list of tables
  • Consistent chapter numbering and headings

2. Formatting & References

  • Font type, size, spacing, and margins consistent
  • APA / IEEE / Chicago applied consistently
  • All in-text citations included in reference list
  • DOI and publication details where available

3. Methodology & Ethics

  • Clear research design justification
  • Sampling method and instruments described
  • Ethical approval / consent statements (if required)

4. Results & Discussion

  • Tables and figures properly labeled and referenced
  • Results aligned with research questions/hypotheses
  • Interpretation avoids unsupported claims

5. Final Submission Review

  • Supervisor approval page included
  • Annexures labeled and referenced in text
  • Similarity report reviewed as per policy
