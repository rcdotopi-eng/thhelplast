Research Glossary

Clear definitions of common research and methodology terms used in BS, MS, and PhD theses.

Validity

The extent to which a research instrument or design measures what it intends to measure.

Reliability

The consistency of a measurement instrument when repeated under similar conditions.

Sampling

The process of selecting a subset of participants from a population for research.

Regression Analysis

A statistical method used to examine the relationship between dependent and independent variables.

Plagiarism

The use of another author’s work or ideas without proper acknowledgment.

Literature Review

A structured synthesis of existing research relevant to a study’s topic and objectives.
